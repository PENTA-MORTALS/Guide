Sign Up/ Sign In

Link: https://signinup.vidhyalakshmi.repl.co/


The above link takes you to the sign up/ sign in page where you have enter the required credentials to register for the first time or to enter in again.
The requirements for signing up manually are :
1. Name
2. Email
3. Password
The requirements for signing in manually are:
1. Email
2. Password
You can also sign up/sign in using Instagram or Google account.
The layout is so simple and straight forward. You can switch between them by clicking the button. Once you enter in the credentials you will taken to your "Personal Dashboard".

Payment

Link: https://payment.aatiq0hussain.repl.co/

This above link will take you to the payment page. The navbar of the page contains link to pages mentioned below.
--> Dashboard
--> Order Booking
To pay you have to enter the following details:
--> Card number
--> Name
--> CCV (pin)
Once you press the arrow, your order is placed. It will take you to the order confirmed page and the water order will be delivered soon to your doorstep. You can press the return button to return to the home page.
